Our Story

About Crispy & Crunch

Crispy & Crunch was born from a simple dream — to bring premium-quality fast food to Bihar at prices everyone can afford. Located opposite SBI Bank in Mahua, we've become the go-to destination for students, families, and food lovers across the region.

From our signature crispy broasted chicken to loaded pizzas and zinger burgers, every item is crafted with fresh ingredients, hygienic preparation, and a whole lot of passion.
